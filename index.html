I’ve been designing for a looong time. I’ve gone from making album covers for my band as a teenager, through creating a visual identity for my university’s radio station, to social media and desktop publishing in the last few years.

I have a bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Goldsmiths, University of London, and I’ve also studied Creative Writing at Central Saint Martins, UAL; Art Direction and Copywriting at SCA 2.0; and an MA in Contemporary Art Theory, again in Goldsmiths.

During the 2020 lockdown, I decided to try my hand at web design and programming, so I completed a Codecademy course in Frontend development. And although bringing websites to life is fun, what I enjoy most is creating beautiful, functional and memorable designs. So after several years, I am back to basics, working with graphic design, learning 3D, and exploring the use of generative AI in photography, adding to my wide array of creative tools.
